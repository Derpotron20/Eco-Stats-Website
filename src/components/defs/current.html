Current

  • Cash Rate Corridor:

    • The ceiling and floor of the cashrate that lenders and buyers get exposed to, usually 0.10% above and 0.25% below current cashrate respectively. Despite these being floors they can also go higher/lower changing how normal interest rates do based on risk.

  • Cyclical Component of the Budget:

    • Changes of budget due to change in economic activity.

  • Government Budget:

    • Plan for government expected expenses and revenue next fiscal year.

  • Structural Component of the Budget:

    • Budget outcome if economy was at full employment. Distinct to changes due to cyclical effects.
